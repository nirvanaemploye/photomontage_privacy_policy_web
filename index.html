Privacy Policy for Photomontage

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

At Photomontage, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

Photomontage does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to use the app.

1.2 Photos and Media

The app requires access to your device's photo library and camera to enable photo editing functionality. All photos and images you work with remain on your device. We do not upload, store, or share your photos on any external servers.

1.3 Usage Data

We do not collect any analytics, usage statistics, or tracking data. Your use of the app is completely private.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information or usage data, there is no information to use, share, or process. All editing operations are performed locally on your device.

3. Data Storage and Security

All your photos, edits, and projects are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your data, and we do not store any information on external servers. You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app or managing your device storage.

4. Third-Party Services

Photomontage does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. When you share your creations to social media platforms (Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook), you are subject to those platforms' privacy policies.

5. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: To capture new photos for editing
  • Photo Library: To access and edit your existing photos
  • Storage: To save your edited images

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and do not enable any data collection.

6. Children's Privacy

Photomontage does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's support section or via the app store listing.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete from our end. You maintain complete control over all your photos and projects on your device.

10. Consent

By using Photomontage, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
